Is It AI? does not collect, store, or require any personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, physical address, or precise location. You can use the application completely without creating an account.

Uploaded images are used solely to provide the AI detection service. We do not use your content for training public AI models, advertising, or tracking purposes.
